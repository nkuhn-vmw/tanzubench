# Company History: Verdant Systems

## Founding and Early Years (2008–2013)

Verdant Systems was incorporated in March 2008 in Austin, Texas, by two co-founders: CEO Dana Kowalski and CTO Reza Shirazi. The company's initial product was a real-time energy monitoring platform for commercial buildings, sold as a subscription SaaS service. By 2011 Verdant had 40 employees and $6M in annual recurring revenue.

The engineering organization in this period was flat: all engineers reported directly to Shirazi. Four senior engineers — Marcus Webb, Priya Nakamura, Tom Obasi, and Lin Fang — effectively led product lines without formal management titles.

## Series B and Expansion (2014–2017)

A $22M Series B in 2014 funded a rapid hiring wave. Verdant's headcount grew from 40 to 180 employees over three years. To manage scale, Shirazi formalized the engineering hierarchy. Webb was named VP of Platform Engineering, Nakamura became VP of Data Science, and Obasi was promoted to VP of Product Engineering. Lin Fang declined a VP role and left the company in 2015 to found a startup.

Under this structure, Webb's Platform team owned infrastructure and APIs; Nakamura's Data Science team owned the analytics and ML pipelines; Obasi's Product Engineering team owned client-facing features. All three VPs reported directly to Shirazi.

## Reorganization of 2024

In early 2024 Verdant's board brought in a new CEO, Simone Reyes, replacing Kowalski who transitioned to a board seat. Reyes commissioned an organizational review that concluded the three-VP structure created siloed decision-making and slowed product releases.

The 2024 reorganization, announced in March, dissolved Obasi's Product Engineering VP role. Obasi's team of 34 engineers was divided: 20 engineers moved to Webb's Platform group, and 14 engineers moved to Nakamura's Data Science group. Obasi himself was offered a principal engineer role within Webb's organization, which he accepted. The reorganized company retained only two engineering VPs: Webb and Nakamura, both still reporting to Shirazi.

## Current Structure (2025)

As of 2025, Verdant employs approximately 220 people. Webb leads a combined Platform and Product team of 85 engineers. Nakamura leads a Data Science organization of 60. Shirazi remains CTO, and Reyes has stabilized revenue growth at 28 percent year-over-year after a flat 2023.
